FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Do I need to connect my wallet?▾
No. We only analyze public blockchain data using your public address. We never ask for private keys, seed phrases, or wallet connections.
Is the scan really free?▾
Yes. The surface scan is completely free and requires no registration. The detailed full AML report with exact percentages is available as a paid upgrade.
Which blockchains are supported?▾
We support 50+ networks including Bitcoin, Ethereum, USDT, TRON, Solana, BNB Chain and many more.
What data sources do you use?▾
We cross-check against OFAC, EU and UN sanctions lists, known mixer and scam address feeds, and analyze on-chain transaction history and counterparties.
